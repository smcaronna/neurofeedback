Meet Marianne

Marianne Becker, LCSW-C

Marianne has been a psychotherapist since 1991 and a neurotherapist since 2001. She has extensive experience in treating emotional trauma, anger, attachment issues, ADD/ADHD, anxiety, and mood issues. Ms. Becker is a pioneer and expert in the use of EEG neurofeedback and provides neurofeedback consultation to other professionals. She also uses neurotherapy extensively to support persons in recovery from drug and alcohol addictions.

She is a co-founder of Attachment Disorder Maryland and has presented at national conferences related to neurofeedback, childhood trauma, and attachment disorders. Ms. Becker uses EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing), attachment therapy and EFT, as well as visualization and imagery technques.

30-40% of Marianne's neurofeedback clients have been referred to her from other psychotherapists. She sees the individual for neurofeedback once or twice a week, while the clients continue their psychotherapy with their original therapist. Marianne works collaboratively with the referring therapist on behalf of the client.

Marianne will partner with group mental health practices as well as addiction programs to provide neurofeedback to their clients at their facilities. Please call (410) 960-0191 for information on this service.
